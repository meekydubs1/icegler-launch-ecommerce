The signature device

The iceblock.

A pair of Icegler frames encased in, or resting on, a literal clear block of ice. Translucent, slightly wet, light passing through — with the organic imperfections of the real thing: air bubbles, faint fracture lines, a soft melt at the edges.

Found in nature and lit beautifully. Never carved, never rendered like stone. This is the recurring hero motif across the brand.

06

Voice

Spare. Confident.
A touch poetic.

i

We come from a place where the light is rare and the cold is honest.

Lets the product and the light speak. Never salesy, never exclamatory.

ii

Short sentences. One evocative word set in italic serif.

Fraunces italic carries a single emotive word inside a display line: light, ice, dusk.

iii

Named for ice and light — The Solstice, The Drift, The Basalt.

Product naming: The ___ plus a single evocative ice or light word. Colourways in the same register.
