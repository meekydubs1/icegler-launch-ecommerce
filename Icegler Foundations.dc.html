01 — Foundations

The system behind the cold.

Icegler makes premium sunglasses designed in Reykjavík and shaped by Icelandic light — the long blue dusk, glacier clarity, black basalt, and the strange warmth of a cold place. This page locks the look.

Glacial Considered Quietly edgy
The signature device

The iceblock.

A pair of Icegler frames encased in, or resting on, a literal clear block of ice. Translucent, slightly wet, light passing through — with the organic imperfections of the real thing: air bubbles, faint fracture lines, a soft melt at the edges.

Found in nature and lit beautifully. Never carved, never rendered like stone. This is the recurring hero motif across the brand.

02 — Colour
